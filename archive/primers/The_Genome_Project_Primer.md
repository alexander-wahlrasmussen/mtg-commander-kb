# The Genome Project — Pilot Primer

**Commander:** Kuja, Genome Sorcerer (Rakdos / BR) · flips to **Trance Kuja, Fate Defied**
**Archetype:** Wizard-token spellslinger burn · **Bracket:** 3
**Full detail:** see `The_Genome_Project_Summary.md`. This page is for piloting at the table.

---

## The pitch

Kuja makes a 0/1 Wizard token every end step. Each Wizard pings **every opponent for 1 whenever you cast a noncreature spell**. Get to **4 Wizards (Kuja counts)** and he flips into Trance Kuja, who **doubles all Wizard damage**. Then you chain cheap spells — draw, rituals, cantrips — and burn the whole table at once.

## How you win

- **Ping storm (primary):** Trance Kuja + 4 tokens = **8 per opponent per noncreature spell**. Chain 3–5 cheap spells in one turn and the table dies. Birgi and Storm-Kiln Artist refund mana so the chain keeps going.
- **Multiplier kill:** add a trigger-doubler (**Harmonic Prodigy / Roaming Throne**) and/or **City on Fire** (×3 to *all* your damage). With both, **one or two spells** is lethal.
- **Backups** when the board's been swept: **Aetherflux Reservoir** (chain to 50+ life, laser a player), **Exsanguinate** after big mana (Mana Geyser / Jeska's Will), or replay your graveyard for one explosive turn with **Underworld Breach / Bonus Round / Dawn Warriors' Legacy**.

## Mulligan — what to keep

Looking for: **3–4 lands + early mana (rock or ritual) so Kuja lands on turn 4**, plus at least one **card-draw** or **Wizard-maker**.

- **Keep:** lands + ramp + a draw spell (Night's Whisper, Sign in Blood, Necropotence) or an extra Wizard producer. The deck assembles the rest by drawing.
- **Toss:** all-gas/no-mana hands; piles of multipliers with no way to cast Kuja; Kuja with zero follow-up and no protection.
- You **do not** need a multiplier in your opener — tokens + draw will find one.

## Turn-by-turn

1. **T1–3:** ramp and draw. Hold removal for genuine problems; don't over-commit.
2. **T4:** cast Kuja. End step → first Wizard token.
3. **T5–6:** stack Wizards — one per end step, plus Vivi's Persistence, Circle of Power, Cornered by Black Mages, Black Mage's Rod, or Black Market Connections' Mercenary mode. At **4 Wizards he flips.**
4. **Combo turn:** drop a mana engine (Birgi, Storm-Kiln Artist, Bayo/Electro), then chain noncreature spells. Every spell pings every token. Add a multiplier to drop the spell count to lethal. **Ideally flip and win the same turn you commit the board.**

## Don't-miss rulings

- **Kuja is a Wizard** — he counts toward the 4 to flip. So **3 tokens + Kuja = flip.**
- Token pings trigger **on cast** and resolve **before** the spell — the damage happens **even if the spell is countered.**
- Trance Kuja's doubling is a **replacement effect**: doubles *all* Wizard damage, combat included. It **multiplies** with City on Fire (×3) and with trigger-doublers.
- **Harmonic Prodigy / Roaming Throne** add an extra *ping trigger*; Trance doubles the *damage*. Different layers — they stack multiplicatively.
- **Black Mage's Rod:** makes a 1/1 Hero token and auto-equips. The **equipped** creature becomes a Wizard and gains the ping — the token doesn't count as a Wizard until it's equipped.
- **Black Waltz No. 3 pings for 2, not 1** — your highest single pinger. Protect it.
- **Stormsplitter** tokens are Wizards (count toward the flip) but **do not ping** — combat damage only.
- **Black Market Connections → "Hire a Mercenary"** = a 3/2 changeling = counts as a Wizard. Use it when you're one short of flipping.

## Threats & timing

- **Board wipes are how you lose.** Cyclonic Rift / Toxic Deluge / Farewell reset you 3–4 turns. Don't over-extend tokens before you can actually go off.
- **Exiling Kuja** (Swords, Oubliette) is worse than killing him — no commander, no flip. Protect him with Lightning Greaves, Kaya's Ghostform, Not Dead After All.
- **No counterspells.** You interact with removal + **redirects** (Imp's Mischief, Redirect Lightning, Return the Favor). Hold a redirect for your combo turn if the pod packs targeted removal.
- **Graveyard hate** (Rest in Peace, Dauthi Voidwalker) shuts off Underworld Breach / Mizzix's Mastery / Reanimate — your recovery plan folds to it.
