# Lightning War — Pilot Primer

**Commander:** Fire Lord Azula (Grixis / UBR)
**Archetype:** Spellslinger burn · copy-amplified X-spell finish · **Bracket:** 3 (plays at Bracket-4 spirit)
**Role:** the deck you pull to **dictate pod tempo** when you're tired of losing to combo.
**Full detail:** see `Lightning_War_Summary.md`. This page is for piloting at the table.

---

## The pitch

Azula makes **your combat the scariest part of the turn.** Every instant/sorcery you cast while she's attacking is **copied** — instants natively, sorceries once a flash enabler is online — and **Twinning Staff** makes every copy event +1. Point a big X-spell into that and the table dies from a single cast. You set the clock; the pod reacts.

## How you win

- **Copy-amplified X-spell (primary, 2 cards):** Azula attacking + an X-spell = 3 instances (×Twinning Staff). **Crackle with Power** X=4 → ~60 to each opponent = table-wipe lethal. **Comet Storm** and **Electrodominance** are instants → no flash enabler needed (the cleanest 2-card kill). Crackle/Banefire are sorceries → need a flash enabler to be Azula-copied.
- **Banefire through the wall:** at **X≥5 it's uncounterable and can't be prevented** — the button for the player hiding behind counterspells.
- **Copy-doublers multiply:** Galvanic Iteration, Increasing Vengeance, Reiterate each stack copies on top of Azula. One X-spell + one doubler in a combat is lethal spread.
- **Graveyard storm (backup):** flash enabler + Yawgmoth's Will / Past in Flames mid-combat replays the yard, each spell copied. Non-infinite but 40+ damage.

## Tutors find the kill

Azula is always in the command zone, so you only need to find **one** finisher. **Emeritus of Woe** (→ Demonic Tutor, any card), **Sanar** (→ Wild Idea, any instant/sorcery), and **Mystical Teachings** (instant-speed) all fetch the missing piece — finisher, Twinning Staff, or a flash enabler.

## Dictating tempo vs the combo pod

- **Burn doubles as removal.** Grand Abolisher is a 2/2 — kill it on sight with Emeritus of Conflict's repeatable Lightning Bolt, Electrodominance, Guttersnipe pings, or any X-spell. **Never let it resolve and lock your turn.**
- **Counter the combo as it's cast.** Your counters are live on opponents' turns *until* an Abolisher is already down — so hold them up and answer the keystone spell. Opposition Agent steals their tutored piece at flash speed.
- **Race, don't grind.** Your job is to kill the table on **your** turn before they assemble. This is a race deck by design — there is no static lock package, and that's the intended build.
- **Ramp is execution-weighted.** Storm-Kiln + Goldspan Treasures, the Blazing Firesinger ritual, Jeska's Will, and Dirgur's cost reduction all spike on the kill turn. Bank Treasures pre-combat, then unload.

## Mulligan — what to keep

Looking for **lands + early ramp + a route to Azula on T4**, ideally with one piece of interaction. A flash enabler or a finisher is gravy — you tutor for whatever's missing.

- **Keep:** ramp/rocks + a counter or two + a path to Azula.
- **Toss:** no-land hands; all-air hands with no early plays.

## Turn-by-turn

1. **T1–3:** ramp (rocks, rituals) and hold interaction up.
2. **T4:** cast Azula.
3. **T5+:** attack, bank firebending + Treasure, cast spells *during combat* for copies; hold counters between turns. Tutor toward a finisher.
4. **Closing turn:** flash enabler up (if using a sorcery X-spell) → cast the X-spell during Azula's combat, chain a copy-doubler / Reiterate, spread lethal. Or Banefire X≥5 at the player behind counters.

## Don't-miss rulings

- **Copies aren't "cast"** — they don't re-trigger Azula or magecraft; X values carry; additional costs paid on the original apply to the copy.
- **Azula only copies while attacking.** Sorcery X-spells (Crackle, Banefire) need a flash enabler to be cast during combat; instant X-spells (Comet Storm, Electrodominance) don't.
- **Twinning Staff adds +1 to every copy event** — Azula makes 2 copies (3 total), and each copy-doubler likewise.
- **Prepared cards** (Sanar, Emeritus of Conflict, Emeritus of Woe, Blazing Firesinger): casting the prepared copy **is a cast** — Azula copies it, magecraft triggers (Storm-Kiln, Guttersnipe), and the creature **stays** afterward.
- **Ozai retains all unspent mana as red** to fund the X-spell mid-combat; firebending mana otherwise vanishes at end of combat.
- **Storm-Kiln Artist makes a Treasure on every cast AND every copy** — a 3-spell Azula combat nets a pile of Treasures.

## Threats & timing

- **Interaction is still your backbone** — 8 counters (3 free: Fierce Guardianship, Force of Negation, Deflecting Swat) + Opposition Agent + removal that doubles as burn. Hold it up on opponents' turns; the deck develops in *your* combat.
- **Rest in Peace / Leyline of the Void** shut off the graveyard-storm backup line — but the primary X-spell kill doesn't need the yard. Fall back on Crackle/Comet/Banefire + copy-doublers.
- **No infinite combo** to be hated out (Aggravated Assault was cut). The kill is a finite copy-scaled X-spell — harder to interact with than a named combo, because it's just "a big burn spell."
