# Lightning War — Pilot Primer

**Commander:** Fire Lord Azula (Grixis / UBR)
**Archetype:** Flash combat spellslinger · infinite-combat closer · **Bracket:** 3
**Full detail:** see `Lightning_War_Summary.md`. This page is for piloting at the table.

---

## The pitch

Azula makes **your combat phase the scariest part of the turn.** While she's attacking, every spell you cast is **copied** — instants natively, everything else once a flash enabler is online — and her firebending adds {R}{R} per attack. Hold counters on opponents' turns, double your own spells on yours, and close with **infinite combats** (Aggravated Assault + Ozai) or a graveyard storm.

## How you win

- **Aggravated Assault infinite combats (primary, 3-card):** Azula (firebending 2) + **Ozai** (firebending 4, *retains all unspent mana as red, indefinitely*) = 6R per combat. Aggravated Assault costs {3}{R}=4 → net **+2R per combat = infinite combats** with evasive attackers. **Ozai is required** — without a mana-retention piece, firebending mana evaporates before you can activate.
- **Graveyard storm:** flash enabler + Yawgmoth's Will or Past in Flames *during combat* replays your whole yard, each spell copied (tripled with Twinning Staff) — 40+ damage in one combat.
- **Jeska's Will during combat:** doubled → a mountain of red mana and free spells, often lethal to one or two players that combat.
- **Hullbreaker Horror soft lock:** cast it in combat, Azula copies it (both stay), then every spell you cast bounces two permanents or counters two spells.

## Mulligan — what to keep

Looking for: **lands + ramp + interaction**, with a path to casting Azula on T4. A flash enabler is great but not required — ~20 native instants/flash permanents already get copied.

- **Keep:** ramp + a counter or two + a route to Azula. Anything that lets you act on opponents' turns.
- **Toss:** no-land hands; all-sorcery hands with no flash enabler and no way to use combat.
- You **do not** need the combo in your opener — you grind card advantage and assemble it.

## Turn-by-turn

1. **T1–3:** ramp and hold interaction up.
2. **T4:** cast Azula.
3. **T5+:** attack with Azula, bank firebending mana, cast spells *during combat* for doubled value; hold counters between turns. You never trade developing for disrupting — development happens in combat.
4. **Closing turn:** assemble Aggravated Assault + Ozai for infinite combats, or storm off with Yawgmoth's Will / Jeska's Will mid-combat.

## Don't-miss rulings

- **Firebending mana lasts only *until end of combat*.** Without Ozai or Leyline Tyrant it's gone before main phase 2 — which is exactly why the sorcery-speed Aggravated Assault needs a mana-retention piece.
- **The copy resolves *before* the original.** Copies are **not "cast"** — they don't re-trigger cast-triggered abilities. X values carry over; additional costs paid on the original apply to the copy.
- **The copy trigger only fires while Azula is *attacking*.** A flash enabler is what lets you cast sorceries/permanents during combat to be copied.
- **Twinning Staff adds +1 copy** per copy effect, so Azula makes 2 copies (3 spells total) — backbreaking on Past in Flames, Yawgmoth's Will, and Jeska's Will.
- **Ozai retains *all* unspent mana** (recolored red) and gains flying + indestructible at 6+ stored. Leyline Tyrant retains red only — not enough for the combo on its own.
- **Irma can copy Ozai** for a second firebending-4 attacker (+4R per combat).

## Threats & timing

- **Interaction is your edge** — 8 counters, 3 of them free (Fierce Guardianship, Force of Negation, Deflecting Swat), plus Opposition Agent to steal a tutored combo piece at flash speed. Hold them up on opponents' turns.
- **During your combat, interaction is doubled** — one removal spell kills two targets.
- **Rest in Peace shuts off the graveyard storm lines**, and you have thin enchantment removal (Vandalblast hits artifacts only; Hullbreaker bounce). Aggravated Assault is a single point of failure — if it's exiled, fall back on the storm/lock lines.
